FF4FE Perfected

...is an open-world romhack and scavenger hunt randomizer forked from Final Fantasy 4: Free Enterprise for Final Fantasy IV for the SNES (originally released in English as Final Fantasy II).

In FF4FE Perfected, you are given the airship at the beginning of the game, and you may complete whatever quests are available to you in any order. Further, the Pass opens a passage in Toroia that will take you directly to Zeromus, whenever you are ready to defeat him. However, unlike Free Enterprise (and in accordance with the good folks at Harp Faders Anonymous, the infamous Harp Check at Cave Magnes is not required. Indeed, there are consequences for those who disobey...

Additional options allow for randomization of key items, characters, treasures, shops, and more.

There are some important gameplay changes from the original game to note; the NPCs in the training room cover the essentials, and a full detailed list of Free Enterprise's changes (on which FF4FE Perfected is based) can be found at the Free Enterprise Wiki.
